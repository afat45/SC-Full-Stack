About Italiania Travel

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Italiania Travel began as a small family-owned business with a deep love for Italian culture and a desire to share it with the world. What started as personalized tours for friends and family has grown into a premier travel service offering unforgettable Italian experiences.

Our journey began in the heart of Tuscany, where our founders, Marco and Sofia, hosted intimate cooking classes and wine tastings in their countryside villa. Word spread quickly about these authentic experiences, and soon requests came from travelers around the world wanting to experience the real Italy - not just the tourist spots.

As we grew, we carefully selected local experts to join our team, each bringing their unique regional knowledge and passion. Today, we offer experiences in every major region of Italy, from the canals of Venice to the cliffs of the Amalfi Coast, always maintaining our commitment to authenticity and quality.

We believe travel should be transformative, not just transactional. That's why we focus on creating meaningful connections between our guests and the local culture, people, and traditions that make Italy so special.

Our team of over 50 local experts includes chefs, sommeliers, art historians, and native guides who are deeply connected to their regions. They're not just employees - they're ambassadors of Italian culture who love sharing their knowledge and passion with visitors.

Every itinerary we create is customized to our guests' interests, whether that's food and wine, art and history, outdoor adventures, or simply soaking in the dolce vita lifestyle. We handle all the details so you can focus on enjoying la bella vita.

Our Mission

To provide authentic, immersive travel experiences that connect visitors with the heart and soul of Italy, its culture, and its people. We go beyond typical tourism to create meaningful, memorable journeys.
